
2D Platform Game Design

Platform Game: This is a type of a video game and sub genre of action games in which core objective is
to move the player between the points in a rendered environment. Platform games are characterized by there
level design featuring uneven terrain and suspended platforms of varying height that requires 
use of the player characteristics such as jumping, climbing, and running to reach the goal.
Example: SuperMario

Introduction:
Players need to collect the coins by avoiding different type of obstacles.  

Steps to Design:

1. Level Design
2. Player Movement
3. Game mechanics (Jump,run,float)
4. To avoid obstacles.
5. To win the goal
